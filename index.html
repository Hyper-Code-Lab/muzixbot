About

MuZix is a Discord music Bot which can play music in your server's voice channel through the YouTube api. Join a voice channel first then type commands in any text channel.

Invite

Invite and start using this bot in your server. Don't forget to share your thoughts with us.

Commands

To play a music you have to type: !!play (anything you want to play)
More commands:
!!search-search and select videos to play
!!playlist-play a playlist from YouTube
!!lyrics-get lyrics of currently playing song
Get a bunch of commands like this by typing: !!help
